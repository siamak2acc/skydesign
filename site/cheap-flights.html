Cheap Flights

Find better fares with flexible planning

Flight prices change with route demand, seasonality, airport choice, and booking windows. Start broad, then narrow the route.

Use flexible dates

Compare several departure and return dates before booking. Midweek flights and shoulder seasons can produce better fares.

Compare nearby airports

Try alternate airports for major cities. A different arrival airport can open up cheaper routes or better connection times.

Watch cabin sales

Economy is not always the only value option. Premium economy and business fares can drop during airline sale periods.
